About

Our goal is to use virtual reality (VR) distraction therapy to better cancer patients' lives. Through our project, patients can experience a virtual reality outside of their daily lives at home or at the hospital, helping them take some time off from their battle against cancer.

Virtual Reality

Using Oculus Rift and Unity, we were able to experiment freely to create beautiful environments for patients.

Bonus

How many cancer cells can you defeat?
Use arrow keys and space bar

In this simple HTML5 game you get to help our Awareness-Ribbon-Bot kill some cancer cells that are in this patient's blood stream, by shooting chemo injection needles at them. How many can you kill? BTW this game never ends and you can't lose. This is because this game symbolizes our continous battle against cancer and our determination to never lose (and not because we didn't have time to integrate any decently interesting mechanics into the game).

Not available on mobile.
